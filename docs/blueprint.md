# **App Name**: WashWise

## Core Features:

- Business Registration: Allow car wash owners to register their business, including address, services, pricing and hours.
- Real-Time Availability Updates: Enable car wash owners to update waiting times and availability in real-time.
- Car Wash Locator: Display a map showing nearby car washes with real-time availability and wait times.
- Smart Scheduling Tool: An AI powered tool that recommends the best times for users to visit car washes based on historical data to avoid peak congestion, including day of the week and time of day.
- Client Dashboard: Provide users with a dashboard to view nearby car washes, manage preferences, and see recommendations.
- Owner Management Area: A dedicated area for car wash owners to manage their business information, update availability, and view analytics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky Blue (#7AD7F0) evoking cleanliness and freshness.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F0F4F7), providing a clean, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant Cyan (#42C9FF) for interactive elements and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for a modern, objective feel.
- Use clean, line-based icons to represent car wash services, locations, and waiting times.
- Implement a card-based layout for displaying car wash information, with clear visual hierarchy.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance user interaction, such as map markers appearing and disappearing, or loading indicators.
